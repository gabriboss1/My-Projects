About Me

Welcome to the Map of Middle Earth! I'm a passionate fan of J.R.R. Tolkien's works and aim to create a detailed, interactive experience for exploring the lands of Middle Earth. Whether you're following the paths of the Fellowship or discovering key events and quests, my goal is to bring Tolkien's world to life in a way that engages and educates.
